My academic hat

How global teams resolve problems using creativity

Technology is constantly affecting how we live. It enables us to collaborate with others anywhere in the world. Such communication and collaboration enable us to innovate like never before. The question is, why do we keep working in offices? Why limit ourselves to expensive, boring offices that make people less efficient?

Are teams that work from home...

...as creative...

...as traditional, office-based teams?

...and therefore as good at identifying and resolving problems?

Not just work: I'm a keen climber, cyclist, musician and photographer

Climbing

I take every opportunity I can to get out climbing in the local area. I love the freedom that trad climbing provides, letting you explore the landscape your own way, without leaving a trace.

Cycling

I have been cycling since I was four and have never stopped. These days I am often to be found out around Lancaster on my trust steed. Or when the weather isn't so good following the cycling calendar.

Music & Conducting

I started to learn the bassoon when I was nine and I am still playing all this time later. Recently I have sought help the musical community that has given me so much by learning to conduct.
